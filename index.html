1. COMPANY OVERVIEW

DomiHive Elite Property Solutions Ltd is a Nigerian ProTech (Property Technology) startup building a unified digital ecosystem for renting, buying, managing, and maintaining properties.

Launch Strategy

  • Launching first in Lagos
  • Scaling to Abuja, Ibadan, Delta State, Port Harcourt
  • Expanding across Nigeria and globally

Core Mission

To deliver a stress-free, agent-free, professional property experience for tenants, owners, and investors.

DomiHive is solving one of Nigeria's biggest urban problems: the stressful, fraudulent, scattered, agent-dependent property experience.

2. PROBLEM STATEMENT

Problems Tenants Face

  • High/hidden agent fees
  • Multiple agents for one property
  • Fake / unverified listings
  • Landlords not responding to maintenance
  • Fraudulent payments

Problems Property Owners Face

  • Difficulty finding trustworthy tenants
  • No time/experience to manage repairs
  • Fear of bad tenants
  • Diaspora owners suffer from mismanagement
  • No transparent digital reporting

Problems for Real Estate Sellers

  • Unqualified leads
  • Agents inflating prices
  • No standardized sales process
  • No digital buyer pipeline
  • No after-sale relationship
